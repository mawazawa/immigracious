## Direct Competitors

### 1. immigreat.ai
**URL:** https://immigreat.ai
**Focus:** Immigration course platform (NOT interview prep)
**Target Visas:** EB1A, EB2-NIW, EB5, O-1

**Pricing:**
- Individual courses: $299/course
- Bundles: Up to 33% discount
- 3-12 month validity

**Strengths:**
- Established brand ("Trusted by 10,000+ immigrants")
- Comprehensive course catalog (40+ courses)
- Focus on self-filing education
- Bundle pricing strategy

**Weaknesses:**
- NOT interview preparation
- Course-based, not interactive
- No voice/AI simulation
- High price point

**Threat Level:** LOW (different product)

**Why We Changed Our Name:** Domain/brand collision risk. "Immigracious" differentiates.

**URL:** https://immiprep.ai
**Focus:** AI mock interview platform
**Target Visas:** B1/B2 (current), expanding to E2, F1, J1, H1B

**Credentials:**
- Built by former USCIS officers
- 15,000+ interviews conducted (by founders)
- 60+ years combined immigration experience
- 70+ countries served

**Features:**
- AI-powered mock interviews
- Video-based simulation
- 7-day access per purchase
- Multilingual support

**Pricing:** Not clearly listed (requires purchase flow)

**Strengths:**
- USCIS officer credibility
- Professional production (video demo)
- Already launched and operational
- Strong credentials marketing

**Weaknesses:**
- NO marriage-based green card focus

- Video-based, not real-time voice
- Limited access window (7 days)

**Threat Level:** MEDIUM (could expand to marriage visas)

**Our Differentiation:**
- Marriage green card ONLY (niche focus)
- Real-time voice with diarization (couples mode)
- Unlimited practice (subscription vs. per-interview)
