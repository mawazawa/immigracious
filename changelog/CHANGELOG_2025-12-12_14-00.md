## Decisions Made

### D001: Voice AI Stack Selection
**Decision:** Hybrid architecture (Deepgram + OpenAI + ElevenLabs)
**Rationale:**
- Deepgram: Best-in-class real-time diarization ($0.0077/min)
- OpenAI GPT-4o: Fast LLM for adjudicator logic (~$0.06/min)
- ElevenLabs: Most natural TTS ($0.08/min)
- Total: ~$0.15/min (75% cheaper than OpenAI Realtime alone)
**Alternatives Rejected:**
- OpenAI Realtime (simpler but 2x cost)
- Hume EVI 3 (no diarization)
- Retell AI (platform lock-in)

### D002: Design Philosophy
**Decision:** Modern + Friendly + AI-forward + Trustworthy
**Inspirations:** Linear.app, Fey.com, Aceternity UI, Apple HIG
**Rationale:** Immigration is emotional; UX must convey trust while feeling premium
